Methodology: GMV and units for H1 2026 are from the "2026_Sales" sheet in Amazon Sales data-2024 to now.xlsx, filtered to Jan–Jun 2026, Cancelled orders excluded. SKUs are mapped to Style# / Category / Division via "dim_product" in Amazon Follow up_2025-CC.xlsx (exact SKU match, then style-prefix match). SKUs from 2026 launches not yet in the item master (~% of H1 GMV, marked "est.") are categorized from the Amazon listing title instead. Current FBA / AMS 2024 / AMS 2025 are from the "Group sales" and "Style Sales" sheets (AMS = Average Monthly Sales). Months of Cover = Current FBA ÷ AMS 2025. Live FBA sell-through and health status are from the "FBA inv" sheet, snapshot 2026-07-13, used to cross-check the AMS-based projection. Styles not yet present in "Style Sales" are marked "No inventory data."
